Steak

Description

Nothing beats a good peice of meat cooked to medium rare and basted with butter!

Ingredients

  • Good Quality Cut of Meat
  • Butter
  • Seasonings of Choice (Garlic, Salt, Pepper)

Steps

  1. Heat a cast iron skillet on high heat
  2. Add butter and seasoned steak to skillet
  3. After achieving a good crust on one side, flip steak and add butter
  4. Baste to top of the steak with hot butter as bottom cooks on skillet
