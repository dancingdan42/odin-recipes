Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a layered italian pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • Ground Protein (beef, turkey, chicken, lamb)
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Mozzerella Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles until they are soft enough to work
  2. Layer the bottom of the dish with the ingredients (sauce, cheese, noodles, sauce
  3. Repeat until the dish is full
  4. Bake at 350 until the cheese is bubbly and browned
